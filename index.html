How To

Snake collects food pieces by moving using keyboard keys. The goal is to collect as many food pieces as possible without allowing snake to hit borders of game board or collide into itself.

Keyboard

  • UP - "w" or "up arrow"
  • RIGHT - "d" or "right arrow"
  • DOWN - "s" or "down arrow"
  • LEFT - "a" or "left arrow"
  • PAUSE - "spacebar"
